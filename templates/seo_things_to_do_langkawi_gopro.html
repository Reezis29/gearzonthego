Top 10 Activities to Film in Langkawi

Ranked by how epic the footage will look

1
🚠

Langkawi SkyCab (Cable Car)

The Langkawi Cable Car at Gunung Mat Cincang offers some of the most spectacular aerial views in Southeast Asia. With a GoPro or Insta360, you can capture the entire 2.2km journey from Telaga Harbour to the peak at 708m above sea level.

Best for: Insta360 360° Also great: GoPro Hero 13
2
🏝️

Island Hopping Tour

Langkawi's island hopping tours take you to Pulau Dayang Bunting (Pregnant Maiden Lake), Pulau Beras Basah, and the Gua Cerita cave. The turquoise waters and limestone cliffs make for incredible footage. A waterproof GoPro or Insta360 is essential.

Best for: GoPro (waterproof) Also great: Insta360 X3
3
🤿

Snorkeling at Pulau Payar Marine Park

Pulau Payar is Malaysia's only marine park in Langkawi waters. The coral reefs are home to blacktip reef sharks, sea turtles and hundreds of fish species. A waterproof GoPro or DJI Osmo Action captures stunning underwater footage.

Best for: GoPro Hero 13 Also great: DJI Action 5 Pro (20m waterproof)
4
🛵

Motorbike Ride Around Langkawi

Renting a motorbike and exploring Langkawi's scenic roads is a must-do. Mount a GoPro on your helmet or handlebar and capture the lush jungle roads, coastal views and hidden waterfalls. The road to Durian Perangin Waterfall is especially scenic.

Best for: GoPro with helmet mount Also great: DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro
5
🪂

Parasailing at Pantai Cenang

Soar above the Andaman Sea and capture breathtaking aerial views of Pantai Cenang and the surrounding islands. A GoPro strapped to your wrist or chest gives you incredible POV footage from 100m above the water.

Best for: GoPro Hero 11/13 Also great: Insta360 Ace Pro 2
6
🚤

Jet Skiing at Pantai Cenang

High-speed jet skiing on the Andaman Sea is exhilarating — and even better when captured on a GoPro. The waterproof design and HyperSmooth stabilization means you get smooth, exciting footage even at full speed.

Best for: GoPro Hero 13
7
🌿

Mangrove Kayaking Tour

Paddle through the ancient mangrove forests of Kilim Karst Geoforest Park. The narrow waterways, limestone caves and wildlife (eagles, monkeys, otters) make for incredible nature footage. Mount your GoPro on the kayak bow for a unique perspective.

Best for: GoPro with kayak mount Also great: Insta360 X3
8
🌅

Sunset at Pantai Cenang

Langkawi's sunsets are legendary — and Pantai Cenang is the best spot to watch them. Set up your GoPro on a tripod for a time-lapse, or use an Insta360 for a 360° panoramic sunset that you can share on social media.

Best for: Insta360 X5 (360° sunset) Also great: DJI Pocket 3 (cinematic)
9
💦

Durian Perangin Waterfall

Hidden in the jungle of northern Langkawi, this seven-tiered waterfall is one of the island's best-kept secrets. Capture the cascading water and lush jungle with your GoPro — the waterproof housing means you can get right into the spray.

Best for: GoPro Hero 11/13
10
🦅

Eagle Feeding at Dataran Lang

Watch dozens of Brahminy kites (the eagles that gave Langkawi its name) swoop down to catch fish thrown from boats. A GoPro with a zoom lens or a Canon R7 with the Sigma 150-600mm lens captures incredible wildlife shots.

Best for: Canon R7 + Sigma 150-600 Also great: GoPro Hero 13
